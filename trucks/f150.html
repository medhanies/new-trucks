2025 Ford F-150

Description

Towing

The 2025 Ford F-150 boasts a robust towing capacity, with the top-tier configuration featuring a 3.5L EcoBoost V6 engine allowing for a maximum towing capacity of 13,500 pounds, making it capable of hauling large trailers, boats, or heavy equipment, depending on the truck's specific configuration and engine choice

Safety

The 2025 Ford F-150 prioritizes safety with a comprehensive suite of driver assistance technologies, including standard features like pre-collision assist with automatic emergency braking, blind-spot monitoring, lane-keeping assist, and rear cross-traffic alert, all part of the Ford Co-Pilot360 system

Trim Levels

Different trim levels like XL, STX, XLT, Lariat, King Ranch, and Platinum with varying features and luxury levels.

Technology

12-inch touchscreen display, 5G connectivity, Ford Co-Pilot360 safety suite.

Powertrains

Multiple engine choices available, including a 2.7L EcoBoost V6, a 3.5L EcoBoost V6, a 5.0L V8, and a hybrid 3.5L PowerBoost V6

Cab and Bed Configurations

The 2025 Ford F-150 offers three cab configurations: the "Regular Cab" for basic seating, the "SuperCab" with extended rear seating, and the spacious "SuperCrew Cab" which can accommodate more passengers.

Specs

  • MSRP : From $37,350
  • MPG : Up to 23 city / 23 highway
  • Engine : 2.7 L V6, 3.5 L V6, 5.0 L V8
  • Horsepower : 325 to 450 hp
  • Transmission : 10-speed automatic
  • Towing capacity : 7,400 to 10,800 lbs
