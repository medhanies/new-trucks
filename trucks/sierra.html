2025 GMC Sierra

Description

Towing

The Sierra 1500 can tow up to 12,100 lbs and has Trailer Sway Control and a Tow/Haul mode. The top four trims come standard with the Prograde trailering system, which includes an app for customizing trailer profiles and monitoring tire pressure.

Safety

The Sierra 1500 has safety features like forward automatic braking and Lane Keep Assist.

Technology

The Sierra 1500 has a 13.4-inch touchscreen with infotainment and Google compatibility, a 12.3-inch hub, and a 15-inch head-up display.

Powertrains

The Sierra 1500 has multiple engine options, including a turbocharged 2.7-liter four-cylinder, a 355-hp 5.3-liter V-8, and a 420-hp 6.2-liter V-8.

Cab and Bed Configurations

The Sierra 1500 is available in three cab configurations and three bed lengths.

Interior

The Sierra 1500 has a spacious interior with lots of room for passengers. Some models have heated seats, dual zone climate control, and a sunroof.

Specs

  • MSRP : From $38,300
  • MPG : Up to 23 city / 29 highway
  • Engine : 2.7 L 4-cylinder, 3.0 L 6-cylinder diesel, 5.3 L V8, 6.2 L V8
  • Horsepower : 305 to 420 hp
  • Transmission : 8 & 10-speed automatic
  • Towing capacity : 8,700 to 9,500 lbs
