2025 Chevrolet Silverado

Description

Towing

The 2025 Chevrolet Silverado boasts a maximum towing capacity of 13,300 pounds when equipped with either the 6.2-liter V8 gasoline engine or the 3.0-liter Duramax turbo-diesel engine, making it a very capable towing truck

Safety

The 2025 Chevy Silverado comes standard with a suite of advanced safety features including automatic emergency braking, forward collision alert, lane departure warning, lane keep assist, pedestrian detection, and automatic high-beam assist, providing drivers with comprehensive protection on the road.

Technology

The Silverado 1500 offers advanced connectivity options like Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a mobile hotspot. It also has available safety features like lane keep assist, collision warning, and automated emergency braking.

Powertrains

The Silverado 1500 offers a range of engine options, including a turbocharged 2.7-liter four-cylinder, a turbo-diesel V6, and two V8 gas engines.

Cab and Bed Configurations

The 2025 Chevy Silverado cab offers a variety of configurations, including a regular cab seating three passengers, a double cab with more rear passenger space, and a crew cab which provides the most rear legroom

Interior

The Silverado 1500 has a roomy cabin with triple-seal inlaid doors for quiet drives. Higher trim levels offer features like heated front and rear seats, a Bose premium audio system, and a 13.4-inch infotainment system screen.

Specs

  • MSRP: From $37,000
  • MPG : Up to 23 city / 29 highway
  • Engine : 2.7 L 4-cylinder, 3.0 L 6-cylinder diesel, 5.3 L V8, 6.2 L V8
  • Horsepower : 305 to 420 hp
  • Transmission : 8 & 10-speed automatic
  • Towing capacity : 8,900 to 9,500 lbs
